職業安全衛生法-勞工應確實遵守之規定為下列三項:

Occupational Safety and Health Law - The regulations that workers should strictly
abide by are the following three items:
1.應確實遵行雇主訂定之安全衛生工作守則

1. Should really abide by the safety and health work rules stipulated by the employer
2.對於雇主依法規要求所施行之健康檢查有接受之義務

2. Obligation to accept health checkups performed by employers in accordance with
legal requirements.
3.勞工對於雇主依法規要求所施以從事工作與預防災變 所必要之安全衛生教育訓練有接受之義務

3. Laborers are obliged to accept the safety and health education and training
necessary for work and disaster prevention provided by employers in accordance
with laws and regulations.
不遵守或不接受三項規定其中一項者，處新台幣三千元以下罰鍰。

Those who do not comply with or do not accept one of the three regulations shall
be fined up to NT$3,000.
